Predicting Heart Disease- Analysis of Risk Factors with Machine Learning

Developed a machine learning project to predict heart disease risk based on key health factors. Applied data preprocessing, feature engineering, and exploratory data analysis to identify significant risk indicators. Implemented classification models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, etc.) and optimized them for accuracy. Achieved strong predictive performance, providing insights for early detection and healthcare decision-making.
